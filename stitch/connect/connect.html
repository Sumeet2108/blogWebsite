Submissions

menu_book Essays & Longform

We seek deeply considered essays between 1,500 and 4,000 words. Pieces should explore the intersections of culture, ecology, and the human spirit. Avoid strictly academic writing; we favor narrative and reflective tones.

photo_camera Visual Narratives

Photo essays should contain 10-15 high-resolution images accompanied by a cohesive statement (300-500 words). We lean towards analogue aesthetics, slow photography, and contemplative landscapes.

Note: We currently review submissions on a rolling basis. Please allow up to four weeks for a response from our editorial team.
